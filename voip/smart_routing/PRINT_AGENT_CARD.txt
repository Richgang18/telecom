║                          QUICK SETUP STEPS                                ║
╠═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                           ║
║  PC SETUP:                                                                ║
║  1. Download from https://www.linphone.org/                               ║
║  2. Menu → Settings → Accounts → Add Account                              ║
║  3. Enter your credentials (above)                                        ║
║  4. Advanced: Transport=TLS, Port=5061                                    ║
║  5. Verify GREEN status = Registered                                      ║
║                                                                           ║
║  MOBILE SETUP:                                                            ║
║  1. Install "Linphone" from Play Store / App Store                        ║
║  2. Menu → Settings → Accounts → Add Account                              ║
║  3. Enter your credentials (above)                                        ║
║  4. Advanced: Transport=TLS, Port=5061                                    ║
║  5. Enable: Microphone, Notifications, Background                         ║
║                                                                           ║
